Welcome to Egypt

Intro

Egypt, located in northeastern Africa, is renowned for its ancient civilization, with iconic landmarks like the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx. The Nile River has been central to its history. Today, Egypt is a mix of ancient traditions and modern life, with Cairo as its bustling capital. The country faces economic and political challenges but remains influential in the region and beyond.
